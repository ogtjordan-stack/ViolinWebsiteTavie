About Octavia

A performer who elevates every room she enters

Octavia is a British professional violinist with over two decades of performance experience across some of the world's most prestigious venues and events. Classically trained, she holds a Bachelor of Music from the University of Bristol, where she was appointed Leader of the University Orchestra.

Known for her commanding stage presence and polished professionalism, Octavia performs entirely from memory — adapting in real time to the energy of the room, the formality of the event, and the expectations of your guests. Whether she is setting a refined atmosphere for a black-tie gala or delivering a high-energy performance for an awards ceremony, she reads the room and responds accordingly.

Her corporate experience spans major brand events at Westfield London, large-scale functions at ExCeL London for audiences of thousands, exclusive members' clubs including Home House, and a career highlight performing at Blenheim Palace for Salon Privé — the UK's most prestigious luxury automotive event.
