About Octavia

A musician who makes your day unforgettable

Octavia is a British professional violinist with a lifelong connection to music, having played since the age of three. Classically trained and holding a Bachelor of Music from the University of Bristol — where she was appointed Leader of the University Orchestra — Octavia brings both exceptional technique and a genuine love of live performance to every wedding.

She performs entirely from memory, adapting instinctively to the atmosphere of each space and moment. From the tender stillness of a ceremony to the warmth of cocktail hour, Octavia creates the kind of musical experience your guests will speak about long after the day is done.

With extensive experience at venues across England and Europe — from stately homes and historic town halls to exclusive members' clubs — and a rich background performing at diverse cultural weddings including Bollywood, Arabic, and Afghani celebrations, every performance is thoughtfully tailored to you.
